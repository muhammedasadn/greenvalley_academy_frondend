Diploma in Computer Applications

A Diploma in Computer Application (DCA) is a short-term, career-oriented program designed to provide fundamental knowledge and practical skills in computer usage, typically spanning 6 months to 1 year.

  • Basics of Computer
  • Microsoft PowerPoint
  • Fundamentals of Internet
  • Graphic Designing
  • Hardware and Networking
  • Computer programming
  • Microsoft Office Word
  • Microsoft Office Excel

Certificate in Fashion Designing

A Certificate in Fashion Design is a post-secondary, short-term program (typically lasting 6 months to 1 year) that focuses on developing the creative and technical skills necessary for a career in the fashion industry.

  • Create design collection
  • Prototype garment for the collection
  • Prepare tech pack
  • Evaluate design development processes
  • Create pattern of the garment
  • Make correction in the garment
  • Maintain health & safety of area
  • Maintain the work area, tools, machines
