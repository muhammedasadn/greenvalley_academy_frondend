Our Programmes

Programmes Designed
for Every Learner

Two flagship programmes built to develop knowledge, character and capability across different age groups and learning journeys.

5 Years Programme

ILP

Integrated Learning Programme

The Integrated Learning Programme (ILP) is a comprehensive 5-year residential curriculum that blends academic rigour with Islamic values, personal development, and vocational skills. Students emerge as grounded, well-rounded individuals prepared to contribute meaningfully to society.

Each year focuses on a progressive layer of learning — from foundational literacy and numeracy through to advanced research, leadership, and community engagement. The programme balances classroom learning with real-world exposure.

  • Full-time residential 5-year structured curriculum
  • Academic + Islamic studies + life skills integration
  • Personalised mentorship and progress tracking
LEARN MORE

3 Years Programme

ILS

Islamic Life Solutions

Islamic Life Solutions (ILS) is a focused 3-year programme designed to equip students with the knowledge, values, and practical tools rooted in Islamic principles. It prepares graduates to navigate modern challenges while staying anchored in their faith and identity.

The programme blends Quranic studies, Fiqh, Islamic history, Arabic language, and contemporary life skills — producing graduates who are confident, ethical, and community-ready through a blend of classroom instruction and experiential learning.

  • Quran, Fiqh, Hadith and Arabic language curriculum
  • Contemporary life skills and ethical grounding
  • Community engagement and leadership development

ADMISSION PROCESS

STEP ONE

PRIMARY EVALUATION
TEST

Begin your journey by submitting a comprehensive digital dossier detailing your academic history, extracurricular pursuits, and a personal statement of intent. Shortlisted candidates will be invited to sit the Primary Evaluation Test at a designated centre.

SELECTION CAMP &
INTERVIEW

Selected candidates are invited for a curated dialogue with our faculty panel. This is a moment to discuss your aspirations, demonstrate your intellectual curiosity, and showcase the qualities that make you a future GreenValley scholar.
